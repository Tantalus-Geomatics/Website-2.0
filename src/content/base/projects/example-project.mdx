This project involved a complex multi-stage legal survey engagement for a private residential client in Squamish's Garibaldi Highlands neighbourhood. The client acquired a large RS-1 zoned parcel with the intent to subdivide it into two fee-simple lots, register a covenant identifying the septic reserve area, and subsequently create a two-lot bare land strata corporation to facilitate individual financing of each duplex unit.

Tantalus Geomatics managed the complete legal survey workflow from initial LTSA research through to final Land Title Office filings. The project required close coordination with the District of Squamish Development Services, the Ministry of Health (for the on-site septic covenant), and the client's conveyancing lawyer to sequence the three separate legal plan registrations correctly and within the municipality's subdivision approval timelines.
