Navigating the unique terrain and regulatory landscape of the Squamish-Lillooet Regional District requires precise, reliable spatial data. Whether you are planning a residential build in Britannia Beach, a commercial development, or a renovation, a certified topographic survey is the essential first step. The Squamish-Lillooet Regional District enforces strict zoning bylaws and development guidelines, particularly regarding building heights, site coverage, and setbacks. Our BC Land Surveyor (BCLS) certified topographic surveys provide the exact elevation data, boundary lines, and existing site features needed to secure building permits and ensure your design complies fully with local municipal requirements.

Properties near Britannia Creek or on the steep slopes of Howe Sound require precise topographic mapping to address flood hazards, debris flow risks, and slope stability.
