Navigating the unique terrain and regulatory landscape of the City and District of North Vancouver requires precise, reliable spatial data. Whether you are planning a residential build in North Vancouver, a commercial development, or a renovation, a certified topographic survey is the essential first step. The City and District of North Vancouver enforces strict zoning bylaws and development guidelines, particularly regarding building heights, site coverage, and setbacks. Our BC Land Surveyor (BCLS) certified topographic surveys provide the exact elevation data, boundary lines, and existing site features needed to secure building permits and ensure your design complies fully with local municipal requirements.

North Vancouver's steep mountain slopes, dense forests, and numerous creek ravines present significant engineering and zoning challenges. Properties in areas like Deep Cove, Lynn Valley, or Lonsdale require precise topographic mapping to manage steep slope development permits, creek setbacks, and wildfire hazard assessments. Tantalus Geomatics specializes in mapping these complex features, ensuring your project meets all municipal environmental and engineering requirements.
