Navigating the unique terrain and regulatory landscape of the Squamish-Lillooet Regional District requires precise, reliable spatial data. Whether you are planning a residential build in Furry Creek, a commercial development, or a renovation, a certified topographic survey is the essential first step. The Squamish-Lillooet Regional District enforces strict zoning bylaws and development guidelines, particularly regarding building heights, site coverage, and setbacks. Our BC Land Surveyor (BCLS) certified topographic surveys provide the exact elevation data, boundary lines, and existing site features needed to secure building permits and ensure your design complies fully with local municipal requirements.

Furry Creek's dramatic coastal hillside terrain and master-planned community guidelines present unique development and engineering challenges. Properties require precise topographic mapping to manage steep slopes, creek setbacks, and marine boundary determinations.
