Navigating the unique terrain and regulatory landscape of the Village of Pemberton requires precise, reliable spatial data. Whether you are planning a residential build in Pemberton, a commercial development, or a renovation, a certified topographic survey is the essential first step. The Village of Pemberton enforces strict zoning bylaws and development guidelines, particularly regarding building heights, site coverage, and setbacks. Our BC Land Surveyor (BCLS) certified topographic surveys provide the exact elevation data, boundary lines, and existing site features needed to secure building permits and ensure your design complies fully with local municipal requirements.

Pemberton's valley floor and surrounding mountain slopes present distinct agricultural, flood, and geotechnical considerations. Properties near the Lillooet River or in the Pemberton Valley floodplains require precise determination of Flood Construction Levels (FCLs) and natural boundaries. Tantalus Geomatics specializes in mapping these critical features, ensuring your project satisfies the Village of Pemberton's environmental and geotechnical review processes.
