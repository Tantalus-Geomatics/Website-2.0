When launching a multi-family or mixed-use development in <span className="text-brand-green font-semibold">City of North Vancouver</span>, initiating pre-sales early in the development lifecycle is critical to securing your construction financing. To market and sell future strata lots before the building is completed, developers must file a Disclosure Statement with the Superintendent of Real Estate, which includes a **Proposed Strata Plan** depicting the proposed strata lots, limited common property (LCP), and preliminary unit entitlements. These plans are highly regulated under the **Real Estate Development Marketing Act (REDMA)** and carry immense financial weight as they allow developers to enter into binding contracts of purchase and sale prior to completion of construction.
