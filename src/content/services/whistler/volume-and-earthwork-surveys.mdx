An accurate volume calculation relies upon the creation of complex 3D Triangulated Irregular Network (TIN) surface models. To accurately capture this topographical data, our firm deploys a suite of state-of-the-art measurement technologies, including UAV LiDAR and photogrammetry, RTK and Static GNSS, robotic total stations and terrestrial LiDAR scanning. We establish a highly robust primary survey control network utilizing Static GNSS measurements and RTK GNSS Receivers, integrating the site seamlessly into established geodetic datums. For active pits, dense corridors, or areas where satellite signals are obstructed, our survey crews deploy high-precision Robotic Total Stations to capture exact terrain breaks.
