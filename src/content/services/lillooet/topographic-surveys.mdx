Navigating the unique terrain and regulatory landscape of the District of Lillooet requires precise, reliable spatial data. Whether you are planning a residential build in Lillooet, a commercial development, or a renovation, a certified topographic survey is the essential first step. The District of Lillooet enforces strict zoning bylaws and development guidelines, particularly regarding building heights, site coverage, and setbacks. Our BC Land Surveyor (BCLS) certified topographic surveys provide the exact elevation data, boundary lines, and existing site features needed to secure building permits and ensure your design complies fully with local municipal requirements.

Lillooet's semi-arid climate and rugged Fraser Canyon terrain present unique geotechnical and slope stability challenges. Development on hillside properties requires precise topographic mapping and slope analysis to address landslide hazards and erosion control. Tantalus Geomatics provides certified topographic surveys that map these critical terrain features, ensuring compliance with the District of Lillooet's building bylaws.
